## Welcome to Digitzs API

The Digitzs API provides a comprehensive solution for integrating merchant services directly into your application. Enable your users to open merchant accounts in real-time and start processing transactions immediately—all without leaving your platform.

## Why Choose Digitzs?

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="No Abandonment" icon="user-check">
    Account creation, payment processing, and transaction reporting all happen inside your app. Better user experience leads to higher conversions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No Waiting" icon="clock">
    Accounts are created in real-time and merchants can accept payments immediately. No more waiting around for approvals.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No Receivables" icon="split">
    Automatically split the proceeds from any sale to collect your platform or marketplace fees. No more invoicing. Get your piece of the pie today.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No Nonsense" icon="handshake">
    Merchants pay a flat rate of 2.9% + 30¢ per transaction. No surprise fees. No gimmicks. Predictable costs make life easier.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Instant Funding" icon="bolt">
    Merchants can get funds instantly when a customer pays with a qualified debit card. Restrictions apply. Reserves required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Lifetime Revenue" icon="chart-line">
    Payments are now a profit center. Earn income every time a transaction is processed. Making money while you sleep is always nice.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get Your Credentials">
    Contact Digitzs to receive your API key and application ID during onboarding
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Follow our [authentication guide](/authentication) to generate your API tokens
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Merchant">
    Use the [Create Merchant endpoint](/api-reference/merchants/create-merchant) to set up your first merchant account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Process a Payment">
    Start accepting payments using our [payment endpoints](/api-reference/payments/overview)
  </Step>
</Steps>
